About Me

Aspiring attorney with roots in advocacy and policy.

I'm Kiana Valtierrez, a bilingual Political Science student at UTEP with ambitions to practice law. Working as a Climate Fellow with the City of El Paso gave me a real look at how legal frameworks, public policy, and community input shape people's day-to-day lives. That experience is what pushed me toward law school.

I'm drawn to public interest law and environmental policy. My background in data research and community organizing gives me a practical lens that I think carries over well into legal work. I'm also bilingual in English and Spanish, which matters a lot in a border city like El Paso.
